**Pixels2Pixels Studio** is excited to announce our participation in **Game Developers Conference (GDC) 2025**, the world's largest and most influential event for the gaming industry. This year, our CEO Djordje Djordjic is traveling to San Francisco to join leading professionals in game development, XR technologies, and 3D animation to explore the latest innovations and opportunities in interactive entertainment.

At GDC 2025, we will showcase our expertise in game development and immersive experiences, positioning Pixels2Pixels Studio as a trusted outsourcing partner in the gaming industry.

## Our Key Goals at GDC 2025

- **Connecting with game studios** looking for high-quality outsourcing services in Unreal Engine, Unity, Godot, and 3D animation.
- **Engaging with publishers and investors** interested in supporting our current and upcoming game projects.
- **Exploring XR and enterprise solutions**, leveraging our expertise in VR/AR experiences and real-time 3D development.
- **Networking with industry leaders**, exchanging ideas, and keeping up with emerging trends shaping the future of gaming and interactive technology.

## Excited to Meet Keisha Howard (SUGAR GAMERS Labs)

A highlight of our visit will be an in-person meeting with **Keisha Howard** from SUGAR GAMERS Labs. We look forward to discussing our collaborative project and exploring new ideas in interactive entertainment and gaming innovation.

## Let's Connect at GDC 2025!

If you are a game studio, publisher, investor, or XR innovator attending GDC 2025, we would love to connect and explore potential collaboration opportunities.

Our team specializes in:

- **Game Development** (Unreal Engine, Unity, Godot)
- **XR Solutions** (VR, AR, Mixed Reality)
- **High-Quality 3D Animation & Asset Creation**

Pixels2Pixels Studio is ready to share ideas, discuss new projects, and build strong industry partnerships. We look forward to seeing you in San Francisco! To schedule a meeting or connect, reach out to us or find Djordje Djordjic at the event.
