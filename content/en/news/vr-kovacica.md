On March 9, 2025, the Gallery of Naive Art in Kovacica witnessed a milestone event — the official launch of **VR Kovacica**, a groundbreaking virtual reality experience that brings the vibrant world of naive art to life. This project allows visitors to step inside stunning paintings, immersing themselves in the rich cultural heritage of Kovacica, a community globally recognized for its unique artistic style.

For the first time, audiences could experience naive art beyond the canvas, interacting with the digital world in a way that blends tradition with cutting-edge technology. The event demonstrated the transformative power of VR, offering a new dimension for storytelling, education, and cultural preservation.

## Couldn't Attend the Launch? You Can Still Experience VR Kovacica!

For those who missed the opening event, VR Kovacica is now a **permanent installation** at the Gallery of Naive Art in Kovacica. Visitors can explore this interactive VR experience anytime, stepping inside paintings and discovering the artistry and storytelling that define Kovacica's artistic legacy.

## What's Next for VR Kovacica?
